Official髭男dism, stylized as Official HIGE DANdism, is a Japanese pop band formed in Shimane, Japan in 2012 by Satoshi Fujihara (lead vocals, piano), Daisuke Ozasa (lead guitar, backing vocals), Makoto Narazaki (bass, saxophone, backing vocals) and Masaki Matsuura (drums, percussion, backing vocals).

'Hige' translates to moustache, and Dandism is Manly, of man. So in English roughly translates to 'Manly Moustache'.
